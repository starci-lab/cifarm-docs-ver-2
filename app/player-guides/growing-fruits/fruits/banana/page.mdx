# Banana

> [!NOTE]
>
> Regular fruit available at Level 10

## Overview

The Banana is a reliable fruit crop in CiFarm, available to players who have reached Level 10. It offers a good balance of growth time and rewards, making it a popular choice for regular farming.

## Growth Details

- **Price**: 5,000 gold
- **Sell Price**: 2,500 gold
- **Young Growth Time**: 12 hours
- **Mature Growth Time**: 6 hours
- **Fertilizer Required**: Every 8 hours
- **Harvest Quantity**: 20 bananas
- **Experience**: 
  - Basic: 12 XP
  - Quality: 24 XP

## Growth Stages

<GrowthStages type="fruit" name="banana" stages={5} />

1. **Seedling** 🌱
   - Initial planting
   - Requires fertilizer

2. **Young Growth** 🌿
   - Development phase
   - Fertilizer needed every 8 hours

3. **Mature Growth** 🌳
   - Main growth phase
   - Can become buggy

4. **Pre-Harvest** 🌸
   - Final development
   - Highest bug risk

5. **Harvest Ready** 🍌
   - Ready for collection
   - Can yield quality fruits

## Tips

- Regular fertilization is crucial for optimal growth
- Watch for bug infestations in mature stages
- Quality fruits provide double experience
- Can be sold in the market for profit
