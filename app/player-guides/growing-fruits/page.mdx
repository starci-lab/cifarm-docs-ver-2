## Growth Stages

Fruits in CiFarm go through 5 distinct growth stages:

1. **Seedling Stage** 🌱
   - Initial planting phase
   - Requires regular fertilization

2. **Young Growth** 🌿
   - Development phase
   - Needs fertilizer every 8 hours

3. **Mature Growth** 🌳
   - Main growth phase
   - Can become buggy (requires treatment)

4. **Pre-Harvest** 🌸
   - Final development stage
   - Highest risk of bugs

5. **Harvest Ready** 🍎
   - Ready for collection
   - Can yield regular or golden fruits

## Growth Times

### Regular Fruits
- Young Growth: 12 hours
- Mature Growth: 6 hours
- Fertilizer Required: Every 8 hours

### NFT Fruits
- Young Growth: 18-24 hours
- Mature Growth: 9-12 hours
- Fertilizer Required: Every 8 hours

## Rewards

- **Regular Harvest**: 20 fruits per harvest
- **Golden Fruits**: ~10% chance (scales with rarity)
- **Weekly Harvest**: Once per week for NFT fruits
- **Season Challenge**: Collect 20 Golden Fruits to complete Season 1 Challenge

## Special Features

- **Quality Yield**: Chance for higher quality fruits
- **Disease Resistance**: Reduces chance of bugs
- **Growth Acceleration**: Speeds up growth time
- **Harvest Yield Bonus**: Increases harvest quantity
